# NorthStar Equipment Group

## Company Overview

NorthStar Equipment Group is a fictional North American company specializing in heavy equipment rental, fleet leasing, equipment sales, and maintenance services.

This project uses NorthStar as the reference business for building a complete enterprise analytics platform.
